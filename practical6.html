Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The Lion of Maharashtra

About

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a great Maratha warrior and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India in 1674. Known for his valor, military tactics, and administrative skills, Shivaji Maharaj was one of the greatest kings in Indian history. His leadership and progressive rule left a legacy that continues to inspire generations.

Major Achievements

  • Founded the Maratha Empire in 1674
  • Established a strong navy to secure the coastline
  • Promoted regional languages and cultures
  • Developed a strong and fair administration system
  • Built numerous forts including Raigad, Pratapgad, and Sinhagad

Legacy

Shivaji Maharaj is remembered not only for his military conquests but also for his vision of Swarajya (self-rule). His ideals continue to be a source of pride for India and especially the people of Maharashtra.
